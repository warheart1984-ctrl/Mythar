## Planned review tracks

The shared compiler pipeline is language-neutral. Contracts may be added for French, Portuguese, German, Italian, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Mandarin Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and other languages after native-speaker or qualified reviewer validation. Reconstructed or historical-language contracts require a **Hypothesized** classification unless separately supported by evidence.

"All major languages" is therefore a governed coverage program rather than an unreviewed claim of complete translation support.
